Our Foundation

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit." — Matthew 28:19

Born in Bonou (Ouémé), MAPR is not just a local church, but a vast network for church planting and humanitarian assistance. Under the vision of Prophet Ganiou Luc Yessoufou, we identify unreached areas, prepare the harvest, plant churches, and provide concrete support to forgotten communities.

The 4 Pillars of the Mission

A unique system called WSFS (Workers, Solidarity, Forgotten, Sentinels), based on four complementary groups working together to fulfill Christ's Great Commission.

🌾

The Workers

Harvest & Intercession

156 couples praying day and night across the country to identify areas where the Gospel has not yet been preached.

🤝

Solidarity

Mutual Aid & Entrepreneurship

An entrepreneurship and contribution model to fund the mission and lift Christian families out of poverty.

💙

The Forgotten

Social Action & Salvation

Field teams that evangelize and respond to social emergencies: orphans, the destitute, abandoned families.

🛡️

The Sentinels

Support & Partnership

Our donors and international partners who make this miracle possible, supported spiritually in return.
